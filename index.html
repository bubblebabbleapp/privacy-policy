3. Information collected automatically by third parties

While we do not collect personal data directly, third-party services integrated into the App may automatically collect certain technical and usage information. This may include:

  • Device information (device type/model, OS version, device identifiers such as Advertising ID (IDFA on iOS / AAID on Android) where permitted by the platform and user consent);

  • Crash and performance data (error logs, stack traces, device configuration);
  • Network and log data (IP address, timestamps).

These data are collected by third-party providers under their own privacy policies (links below). We use such data solely for analytics, crash fixing, ad delivery and measurement, and product improvement.

Third-party services used:

5. Advertising, tracking and choices

Advertising identifiers & ATT (iOS): On iOS, the App may access the advertising identifier (IDFA) only if you permit tracking via the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt. If you deny consent, the advertising identifier will not be available for personalized advertising.

Personalized ads & opt-out: To limit interest-based ads served by Google, users may visit Google’s ad settings.

Device controls: You can reset or limit advertising identifiers in your device settings (iOS and Android). These actions affect ad personalization across apps.

Cookies & local storage: The App itself does not set browser cookies. Third-party SDKs may store identifiers or local data for analytics and ad delivery as described in their policies.
